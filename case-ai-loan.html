AI-Assisted Loan Approval Platform

Role: Product Manager
Domain: Retail Banking (Mid-size Bank)

1. Context & Background

Mid-size retail banks compete heavily on loan approval speed, especially for personal and MSME loans. However, regulatory requirements, legacy core banking systems, and risk controls make full automation impractical.

At this bank, loan approvals relied on semi-manual processes that created delays, operational overhead, and inconsistent customer experiences across branches.

2. Problem Statement

  • Loan approval turnaround time (TAT): 3–5 working days
  • High manual effort for low-risk applications
  • Inconsistent approval decisions across branches
  • Poor transparency for customers during the approval journey
Core PM Question: How can we significantly reduce approval time without increasing credit risk or violating regulatory expectations?

3. Users & Stakeholders

  • Primary: Retail banking customers
  • Secondary: Credit officers
  • Stakeholders: Risk, Compliance, Operations teams

4. Goals & Success Metrics

North Star Metric: Loan Approval Turnaround Time (TAT)

Supporting Metrics

  • Straight-through processing (STP) rate
  • Approval consistency across branches
  • Customer drop-off rate

Guardrail Metrics

  • Default rate (30 / 60 / 90 DPD)
  • Manual override frequency
  • Audit observations

5. Discovery & Key Insights

  • ~80% of applications followed predictable, low-risk patterns
  • Credit officers wanted decision support, not automation
  • Risk teams rejected black-box AI models
  • Explainability was more important than model accuracy
Insight that shaped the product: Trust and explainability would drive adoption more than automation.

6. Solution Design

Designed a hybrid decisioning platform that combines rules, AI assistance, and human oversight. 

Customer Application
   ↓
Rule-Based Eligibility Checks
   ↓
AI Risk Score (Explainable)
   ↓
Credit Officer Review (if required)
   ↓
Core Banking System (T24)

Why this approach?

  • Meets regulatory expectations
  • Builds trust with credit officers
  • Reduces manual effort on low-risk cases

7. Product Requirements (PRD – Summary)

Objective

Reduce loan approval turnaround time by 30–40% without increasing credit risk.

In Scope

  • Personal loans
  • Configurable eligibility rules
  • AI-assisted risk scoring (recommendation only)
  • Manual override with audit trail

Out of Scope

  • Fully automated approvals
  • Black-box ML models

Non-Functional Requirements

  • Explainability
  • Auditability
  • High availability

8. MVP Scope & Roadmap

Phase 1 (MVP)

  • Personal loans only
  • Rule-based eligibility
  • AI score as read-only recommendation

Phase 2

  • MSME loans
  • Configurable risk thresholds
  • Branch-level analytics

Explicit Non-Goals

  • Real-time bureau integrations
  • Automated rejections

9. Analytics & Measurement

Event Tracking

  • Application submitted
  • Eligibility passed / failed
  • AI score generated
  • Override applied
  • Approval / rejection completed

Decision Triggers

  • Override rate > 25% → Review rules
  • Default rate increase → Tighten thresholds
  • Drop-offs increase → Simplify journey

10. Outcome & Learnings

  • Projected 30–40% reduction in approval TAT
  • ~20% reduction in manual processing effort
  • No increase in default rates
  • Explainability significantly improved adoption

11. Key Learnings

  • In regulated fintech, trust beats sophistication
  • Metrics alignment before launch is critical
  • Human-in-the-loop accelerates adoption
